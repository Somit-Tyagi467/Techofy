IQOO Z6 5G Details

  • IPS LCD, with 120Hz refresh rate
  • Triple camera array on the rear side
  • 16MP Front Camera with Screen Flash
  • 18W Fast Charging
  • 128GB Internal storage
  • Qualcomm Snapdragon 695 chipset
  • 4GB RAM with extended RAM 2.0
  • Side-mounted fingerprint sensor

iQOO Z6 5G is quite a sorted offering overall, equipped with a vivid display profile, great cameras and Fast Charging compatible battery panel under the hood. Also, this particular variant flaunts the latest Android v12 OS accompanied by a 4GB of RAM.

iQOO Z6 5G offers 6.58 inches IPS LCD having a waterdrop notch at the centre top. This bezel-less front screen also gets a resolution of 1080 x 2408 pixels, an aspect ratio of 20:9 along with a pixel density of 401ppi. Besides, there is a refresh rate of 120Hz.
A 50MP f/1.8 Wide Angle Eye AF Camera is available on the rear side of the iQOO Z6 5G, which is also backed by a 2MP f/2.4 Macro Camera and a 2MP f/2.4 Depth Camera. The brand has also provided a 16MP f/2.0 Wide Angle Camera on the front side for video shoots, selfies and video calls.

The smartphone delivers powerful performance, thanks to its Qualcomm Snapdragon 695 chipset and 4GB RAM (6nm). It is equipped with an Octa-core processor that includes a Dual-core, Kryo 660, 2.2GHz and a Hexa-core, Kryo 660, 1.7GHz layout. Attaining the graphical needs, iQOO has installed an Adreno 619 GPU within Z6 5G.
The day to day operations of the iQOO Z6 5G are supported by a 5000mAh Li-ion battery. It is non-removable by nature and comes compatible with a 18W Fast Charging technology.

The 128GB internal storage loaded within iQOO Z6 5G can be expanded up to 1TB. Connectivity features available with this smartphone are 5G, 4G VoLTE networks, Mobile Hotspot, Bluetooth v5.1, A-GPS with Glonass and USB Type-C.
